Match against Bergvliet (Away) - 13 April 2022

League 12th
Opposition Club Bergvliet
Opponent Peter Hendricks
Result Win
Score 3-1

This was the second match of the season. The thing I liked most about this match was that I came back to win 3-1 after losing my first game. It was a real test of my mental strength and it was a massive boost to my confidence to come out on top.

My opponent was a fair bit older than me, which usually means he'd have had more experience playing the game, but I'd usually be the one with better fitness. I felt we were fairly even skill wise and that he played a lot of risky shots in the first game that paid off for him. This luck didn't last into the rest of the games and I think his fitness let him down which allowed me to take the next 3 games fairly comfortably.

Unfortunately our team ended up losing this fixture based on the number of games, again, highlighting the importance of doing my best to not give any games away.
