Match against Milnerton (Home) - 6 April 2022

League 12th
Opposition Club Milnerton
Opponent Marco Palmieri
Result Win
Score 3-1

This was my first league game ever for squash. I was feeling quite good going into it. Thinking back, I was still probably a little unfit and could've worked on that aspect a little more. But otherwise, I'd say my confidence and skills had improved a lot since starting at the club about 4 months earlier.

I won 3-1, but I felt I probably should've won 3-0 because I was dominating my opponent. I think I became complacent which is why I gave away a game. It's important that I don't do this because this was a close fixture for our team as a whole and it came down to overall points which we luckily won on, but if I hadn't given away a game, it wouldn't have been so close.
