Match against Camps Bay (Home) - 20 April 2022

League 12th
Opposition Club Camps Bay
Opponent Kyle Hobbs
Result Win
Score 3-2

This was the third match of the season. My opponent and I were fairly similar in age, so I couldn't bank on me being younger and more fit to be of any help in this match. In fact, I was probably the less fit player as I was more than 10kg overweight at this point.

The match was a really close one. I ended up winning 3-2. I was 2-0 up after the first two games, but ended up losing the next two. The fourth game was pretty bad. I lost 5-15, so my head wasn't in a good space. After winning in the fifth, I also felt like it was mainly due to my opponent making a lot of unforced errors, so it didn't necessarily boost my confidence as I couldn't attribute the victory to my own skill.

Our team ended up winning this fixture quite comfortably with 3-1 in matches, so luckily didn't need to go into counting games or points for this one.
